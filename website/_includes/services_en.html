Atarau has three service models:

Project Partnerships

We provide technical oversight for joint projects with select partners: NGOs, NPOs, IGOs and governmental organisations.

In-house Development

Atarau develops a number of socially-oriented projects under its own brand through grant funding, government support, sponsorship and the open source community.

Consulting Services

Organisations looking for innovative data solutions engage with Atarau for technical advice and development.
